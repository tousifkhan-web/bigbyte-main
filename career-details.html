About Bigbyte

Bigbyte helps enterprises turn raw data into real decisions. We specialize in AI-powered Snowflake solutions, cloud data engineering, and multi-cloud integration that solve complex, real-world problems across retail, healthcare, finance, and more. We’re a remote-first company that values impact over hours and curiosity over credentials. If you love learning, building, and solving real business challenges with cutting-edge AI tech — you’ll fit right in. Role Overview We’re looking for a passionate and curious AI Intern. This 3-month internship is a hands-on opportunity to work on real-world AI and Automation use cases, from prompt engineering to LLM-based automation — all within a startup-like environment where your ideas matter.

Responsibilities

Assist in designing and testing AI workflows using tools like LangChain, OpenAI APIs, or vector DBs Support prompt engineering, data preparation, and agent orchestration Help document, test, and iterate on internal tools and pipelines

Who You Are

A student or recent graduate in Computer Science, AI/ML, Engineering, or related field. Strong interest in AI, LLMs, and multi-agent architectures. Basic programming skills in Python and familiarity with REST APIs. Eager to learn, experiment, and contribute to an ambitious AI product. Self-driven and capable of working independently in a remote environment.

What You’ll Gain

Deep exposure to cutting-edge AI systems and agentic workflows. Mentorship from engineers working on complex AI architectures. Real product contributions you can reference in future roles. Certificate and Letter of Recommendation upon successful completion. Potential for conversion to a full-time, paid role based on performance.

Note: This is a three-month unpaid internship.

We deeply value your contributions and are committed to providing you with meaningful, hands-on experience that will enhance your resume and professional skill set. This is a fully remote role, and we welcome applications from both students and recent graduates who are willing to dedicate a minimum of 6 hours per day to this opportunity.
