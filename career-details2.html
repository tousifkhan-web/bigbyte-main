About Bigbyte

Big Byte Corporation is a data and AI consulting firm specializing in high-impact Snowflake solutions for enterprises. We combine deep cloud data engineering expertise with advanced AI/ML to help clients unlock real business value. From automating insights to scaling analytics across AWS, Azure, or Data bricks, we enable digital transformation with speed and precision. Our certified team builds reliable systems that solve tough data problems and deliver measurable ROI, with a proven track record in retail, healthcare, CPG, and finance.

Role Description

We are rebuilding our public-facing web presence and design system. You will own the translation of brand and UX direction into performant, accessible, responsive frontend implementations. This role blends UI/UX craftsmanship with modern frontend engineering (semantic HTML, scalable CSS architecture, component-driven JS) to deliver pixel-consistent experiences across devices. It begins as a contract engagement with potential conversion to full-time based on delivery quality, initiative, and ownership.

Responsibilities
  • Audit current site structure, typography, spacing, and component patterns; propose design system improvements.
  • Create high-fidelity mockups / interactive prototypes (Figma or Adobe XD) for new pages and key sections.
  • Implement semantic, accessible HTML5 and modular, scalable CSS (BEM / utility layering) for redesigned pages.
  • Refactor inline / duplicated styles into reusable tokens (colors, typography scale, spacing, breakpoints).
  • Optimize for performance: reduce DOM weight, consolidate CSS/JS, improve Lighthouse Core Web Vitals.
  • Ensure WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility: color contrast, focus states, aria attributes, keyboard navigation.
  • Collaborate with leadership to align visual narrative with brand positioning and messaging hierarchy.
  • Enhance marketing collateral (slide decks, visuals) for consistency with refreshed web style guide.
  • Document patterns and handoff guidelines for future internal contributors.
Who You Are
  • 1.5+ years professional experience shipping responsive marketing or product websites.
  • Portfolio demonstrating end‑to‑end redesigns (before/after) and component/system thinking.
  • Fluent in HTML5, modern CSS (Flexbox, Grid), responsive layouts, and CSS architecture principles.
  • Strong eye for typography, rhythm, visual balance, and interaction micro‑details.
  • Comfortable turning UX wireframes into production-ready, accessible implementations.
  • Self‑directed, communicates clearly, and manages iteration cycles with minimal oversight.
Bonus Points
  • Experience with a component framework (React / Vue) for future progressive enhancement.
  • Design tokens / style dictionary implementation experience.
  • Prior optimization work improving Lighthouse performance & accessibility scores.
  • Illustration / motion design skills (micro‑animations, SVG, Lottie).
  • Basic familiarity with Git version control and structured PR workflows.
What You'll Gain
  • Ownership of a greenfield design system and its public launch footprint.
  • Direct collaboration with founders—fast feedback loops, strategic visibility.
  • Opportunity to define scalable frontend foundations influencing future product builds.
  • Flexible remote environment focused on outcomes over hours.
  • Clear pathway to full‑time conversion tied to quality, velocity, and initiative.
  • Creative latitude to propose and experiment with modern interaction patterns.
