Unlock the full potential of your first-party data without compromising user privacy. Activation Bridge helps advertisers significantly improve campaign performance and Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) by optimizing match rates between your Snowflake environment and major advertising platforms.

We enable Advertisers to securely share Audience and Conversion data with Google and Facebook directly from Snowflake. Unlike legacy activation methods, Activation Bridge leverages Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs) powered by Confidential Compute.

This cutting-edge approach ensures that your sensitive customer data is processed in a secure, hardware-isolated environment. This allows for high-fidelity matching against Google and Facebook users while ensuring that raw Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is never exposed—even during the matching process.

Expected Workflow

01

Data Preparation

Create a view within Snowflake containing your target audience or conversion events (including necessary identifiers like hashed emails, phone numbers, or click IDs).

02

Secure Connection

Establish a secure connection via Activation Bridge to your desired destination (Google Ads, Google Marketing Platform, or Meta Ads Manager).

03

Confidential Matching:

Activation Bridge utilizes a Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) to perform the data handshake. Data is encrypted in transit, processed within the secure enclave for matching, and never visible in plaintext outside your environment.

04

Automated Activation

Matched audiences and conversions are automatically pushed to the respective ad platform for immediate use in targeting or measurement.

05

Performance Feedback

Receive a confirmation log back in Snowflake detailing the number of records sent and the success rate of the transmission, allowing you to monitor match effectiveness.

Why Activation Bridge?

In a post-cookie world, first-party data is your most valuable asset, but privacy regulations and technical barriers often limit its utility. Activation Bridge solves this dilemma by prioritizing security-first interoperability.

By utilizing Google’s Trusted Execution Environment and Confidential Compute standards, we provide a level of data hygiene and security that goes beyond standard hashing. This advanced architecture minimizes signal loss, ensuring that more of your Snowflake data matches actual users on ad platforms. The result is larger addressable audiences, more accurate conversion attribution, and a strictly compliant data workflow that satisfies both InfoSec and Marketing teams.
