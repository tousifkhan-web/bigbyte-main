Role Description

This is a contract remote role for a Branding & Digital Marketing Lead at Bigbyte. This is not a junior role. We're looking for someone who has led digital strategy before. Instead of a traditional interview, we’re flipping the script. Here's how it works: We don’t do traditional interviews. If you think you’re the right fit for this high-ownership role: Submit your Resume Submit a 60-Day Strategic Branding & Marketing Plan for Bigbyte Please add your 60-Day Strategic Branding & Marketing Plan to your resume and send it to us. Show us how you'd lead from Day 1 — your ideas, clarity, and creativity. Do your research. Think like you're already in the role. Only applications with Strategy Doc will be considered. Bonus points for visuals, examples, or references. Share a portfolio or examples of impactful work you’ve done that we can review.

Your document should cover

Which platforms and channels to focus on (LinkedIn? Blogs? Paid Ads? Thought leadership?) What kind of content will get us attention in the B2B AI/Data space? How will you position Bigbyte as a credible brand in a crowded market? How to position a tech consulting brand in a crowded B2B space What metrics matter, and how will you report success? The best strategy document will be personally reviewed by our CEO, and shortlisted candidates will be invited for direct 1:1 discussion. Compensation will be based on the strength of your strategic plan. This is your chance to turn your ideas into a long-term opportunity.

Qualifications

marketing and digital strategy Strong portfolio or proof of work in B2B tech, SaaS, or data/AI space Clear understanding of platform-specific strategies (especially LinkedIn) Ability to write and structure high-converting, professional content Familiarity with tools like Canva, Figma, or any creative suite Experience running organic and paid campaigns that brought real leads Comfortable working independently with founders and leadership team

What You’ll Get

Freedom to own and shape the brand Remote work with complete flexibility A rare chance to be the brain behind a startup’s digital rise Performance-based pay + possibility of full-time conversion if results are delivered

Deadline:

Submit your 60-day strategy by Friday, 12:00 PM IST.
